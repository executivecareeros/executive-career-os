# Executive Reasoning
> Purpose: Explain how Atlas compares executive decisions without pretending certainty.

Atlas assigns Immediate, Soon, Monitor, Future, or No Action priority. It compares Apply, Wait, Reject, Monitor, Negotiate, and Network First. Each alternative lists advantages, disadvantages, risks, and deterministic confidence.

Unknown compensation, recruiter, manager, relocation, reporting line, equity, interview stage, benefits, travel, and board exposure become explicit questions. Answers can change confidence and recommendation; missing information never becomes an invented assumption.
