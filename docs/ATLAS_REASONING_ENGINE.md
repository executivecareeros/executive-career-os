# Atlas Reasoning Engine
> Purpose: Define the deterministic decision-artifact pipeline across Executive Career OS.

The pipeline collects, validates, normalizes, evaluates, prioritizes, detects conflicts and trade-offs, generates alternatives and explanations, then creates an immutable snapshot. Inputs come from confirmed Blueprint, Ledger, Memory, Knowledge, Discovery, Compensation, Application, Company, Opportunity, Principle, and Career Capital records.

Outputs expose evidence, references by domain, rules, conflicts, trade-offs, gaps, questions, assumptions, alternatives, confidence, why, why not, what changed, and what would change the recommendation. The product exposes decision artifacts—not hidden chain-of-thought.

There is no LLM, generated reasoning, chat, prediction, or external model provider.
