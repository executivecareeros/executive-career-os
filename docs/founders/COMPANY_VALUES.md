# Company Values

> **Purpose:** Define the standards used to make product, design, engineering, and company decisions when the answer is not obvious.

## Earn Trust Slowly

Trust is accumulated through reliable behavior, honest limits, and respect for sensitive context. We do not borrow trust from confident language or prestigious technology.

## Serve Executive Agency

We help executives see and decide. We do not create dependency, make choices on their behalf, or optimize for compliance with our recommendations.

## Preserve What Matters

Careers unfold over time. We protect history, rationale, evidence, and learning from silent loss or convenient revision.

## Prefer Evidence to Performance

We would rather be demonstrably useful than appear impressive. Claims, recommendations, and product decisions should withstand inspection.

## Make Complexity Clear

We do not deny complexity; we organize it. Good work reveals the material decision without hiding the evidence underneath it.

## Practice Responsible Restraint

Capability does not create permission. We collect less, automate carefully, and decline work that compromises privacy, dignity, or control.

## Disagree with Respect

Constructive challenge improves judgment. We identify assumptions and trade-offs directly, without hierarchy, performance, or personal attack.

## Build for the Long Career

We choose durable memory, concepts, and relationships over short-term engagement. The next transaction must not weaken the whole career.

## Own Consequences

We remain accountable for the effects of our systems, including foreseeable misuse, confusing language, inaccessible design, and delegated provider behavior.

## Improve Without Rewriting

We learn and correct openly. We do not erase prior decisions to make progress appear cleaner than it was.

## Quality Is Respect

Accuracy, accessibility, security, and thoughtful language respect the executive’s time and the seriousness of the decision.
