# Executive Productivity
> Purpose: Define the deterministic preparation and action layer of Executive Career OS.

The productivity workspace combines Executive Briefs, interview and meeting preparation, application checklists, negotiation planning, decision comparisons, structured notes, tasks, follow-ups, and deadlines. It consumes confirmed domain records and never performs actions automatically.

All durable records are Workspace-scoped, repository-backed, and protected by RLS. Memory-demo content remains explicitly fictional.
