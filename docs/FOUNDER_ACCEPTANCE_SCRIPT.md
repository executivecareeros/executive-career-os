# Founder Acceptance Script

> Purpose: Provide the executable acceptance sequence required before any design-partner invitation.

Use only a fictional `example.invalid` identity in an isolated staging environment. Record the timestamp, build commit, tester, and pass/fail evidence for every step.

1. Create one invitation and verify the secret is shown once.
2. Prove missing, expired, revoked, mismatched, and replayed invitations fail explicitly.
3. Register the exact invited email, verify it, sign in, and complete onboarding.
4. Add fictional professional history, refresh, sign out, sign in, and confirm persistence.
5. Create and revise the minimum Blueprint; prove the prior revision cannot be overwritten.
6. Create one fictional opportunity with company, source, recruiter, constraints, claims, and optional same-currency compensation.
7. Run Atlas and confirm every evidence reference resolves to the current Workspace.
8. Confirm unknown fields remain unknown and no demonstration record appears.
9. Finalize a decision; verify exactly one snapshot, Ledger event, task, audit, and commit.
10. Repeat finalization and verify the stable commit is returned without duplicates.
11. Submit feedback and verify founder-only triage visibility.
12. Submit export and closure requests; verify participant status and duplicate prevention.
13. Attempt cross-Workspace reads and verify denial.
14. Expire the session during a save and verify a recoverable sign-in path with no partial write.
15. Restart the browser and verify the complete saved state.

Founder result: **Not performed**. Design-partner use remains prohibited until every step passes and evidence is attached.
