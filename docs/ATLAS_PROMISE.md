# The Atlas Promise

> Purpose: State the trust commitments accepted before an executive creates their Career Memory.

1. You own your career.
2. Atlas explains every recommendation.
3. Atlas never silently changes your history.
4. Your data is portable through Career Passport.
5. You control who can access your information.

Acceptance is explicit and timestamped. It is not bundled into general account registration and cannot be inferred from product use.
