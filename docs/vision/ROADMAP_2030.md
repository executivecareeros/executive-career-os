# Roadmap to 2030

> **Purpose:** Describe the major long-term capability areas of Executive Career OS without prescribing sprint plans or implementation timing.

## Strategic Foundation

### Executive Blueprint

A living model of executive identity, ambitions, preferences, constraints, principles, and risk tolerance.

### Career Ledger and Career Replay

Permanent historical memory and a coherent narrative of decisions, events, evidence, outcomes, and lessons.

### Atlas

The explainable intelligence and decision-support layer grounded in the Blueprint and career history.

## Intelligence Workspaces

### Opportunity Intelligence

Structured evaluation of roles, fit, strategic value, risk, and career impact.

### Company Intelligence

Longitudinal understanding of target employers, market position, leadership context, relationships, and strategic relevance.

### Compensation Intelligence

Complete memory and decision support across expectations, negotiations, offers, and long-term economic outcomes.

### Interview Intelligence

Preparation, evidence capture, signal interpretation, and learning across executive interview processes.

### Executive Network Intelligence

Relationship memory and thoughtful support for building, maintaining, and activating an executive network.

### Document Intelligence

Versioned, evidence-based management of executive CVs, biographies, board profiles, letters, and supporting materials.

## Operational System

### Applications CRM

Operational continuity from preparation through submission, interviews, offers, decisions, and follow-up.

### Executive Daily Brief

A concise daily synthesis of material changes, approaching decisions, commitments, risks, and opportunities.

### Automation

Controlled, explainable, auditable assistance that follows earned trust and explicit authority.

## Long-Term Intelligence

### Career Capital

A multidimensional view of the assets, credibility, relationships, demand, and momentum accumulated across a career.

### Executive Simulation

Scenario exploration that helps executives consider alternative choices, assumptions, and second-order effects without presenting forecasts as certainty.

### Voice Avatar

A natural conversational interface that reflects the executive’s authorized context while preserving accuracy, privacy, and control.

## Direction, Not a Delivery Schedule

This roadmap identifies enduring capability areas. Sequence and scope should respond to evidence, trust, user value, and technical readiness. It should be revisited as the Career Intelligence Platform category develops.
