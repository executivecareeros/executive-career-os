# Preliminary Trademark Screening

## Scope and method

This is a knockout screen, not a legal clearance opinion. Searches covered exact-name and close-name web results, public trademark-result pages, company directories, Wikipedia, software/company references, GitHub-style developer usage, and app-store-oriented queries. Relevant likely classes include at least Nice 9 (software), 35 (business services), 41 (education/coaching), and 42 (software/SaaS).

## Interpretation

“Low–medium” means only that no obvious knockout conflict appeared in this preliminary search. It does **not** mean registrable, non-infringing, or safe worldwide. Similarity is assessed by sight, sound, meaning, goods/services, channels, and jurisdiction—not exact spelling alone.

## Required attorney search

Counsel should run full searches in the United States, European Union, United Kingdom, Türkiye, Canada, and other launch markets; inspect live and dead records; review common-law use; assess translations/transliterations; and issue a written clearance opinion before incorporation, launch, or domain publicity.

> **Legal disclaimer:** This research provides preliminary availability only. Final trademark clearance must be performed by a qualified trademark attorney before adoption. Final legal clearance is outside the scope of this research.
