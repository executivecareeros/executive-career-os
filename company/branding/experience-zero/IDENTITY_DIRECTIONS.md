# Orendalis Identity Directions

Ten original directions were evaluated. They are references for Orendalis only; none copies another institution.

## 1. The Quiet Observatory — Recommended

- Philosophy: perspective before movement.
- Emotional objective: calm intelligence and private agency.
- Wordmark: restrained uppercase Orendalis with generous tracking.
- Symbol: an eye-like horizon held inside an open circle; perspective, not surveillance.
- Executive seal: the symbol within a fine circular rule with Discretion · Perspective · Agency.
- Typography: humanist sans for action; literary serif for consequential statements.
- Color: obsidian, warm ivory, mineral blue, restrained bronze.
- Motion: short reveals and state transitions; never ambient spectacle.
- Photography: architectural light, distance, thresholds, human presence without performance.
- Illustration: precise horizon, constellation, and evidence-line diagrams.
- Arrival: quiet scale, one truth, one primary action.
- Authenticated experience: dark private office with evidence-led hierarchy.
- Sonic identity: future optional two-note glass-and-wood cadence; muted by default.
- Principles: spacious, truthful, composed, legible, never cold.
- Timelessness: grounded in proportion and material restraint rather than visual trends.
