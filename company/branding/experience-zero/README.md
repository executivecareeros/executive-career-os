# Experience Zero

Experience Zero defines the emotional front door of Orendalis. It governs the public arrival, private access, first authenticated minute, identity, language, motion, and future sonic behavior.

The standard is simple: an executive should feel understood before being asked to act. Orendalis is not presented as a job board, workflow tool, or artificial-intelligence product. It is a private career intelligence institution where consequential decisions receive memory, perspective, and care.

## Current implementation

- A public arrival now precedes authentication.
- Orendalis has a consistent wordmark and perspective symbol.
- Public arrival and private access share one visual and verbal identity.
- Authenticated navigation and the daily brief now continue the same private-institution atmosphere.
- Atlas is introduced as a trusted second mind, never as certainty or automation.
- Motion is brief, purposeful, and disabled when reduced motion is preferred.
- No sound is played. A future sonic signature remains subject to explicit user control and testing.

## Acceptance standard

Within ten seconds: this feels private and distinct. Within thirty seconds: this understands executive responsibility. Within one minute: this protects judgment rather than replacing it. After leaving: the next decision feels worth bringing back.

## Current emotional sequence

1. **Arrival:** recognition before explanation.
2. **Purpose:** responsibility and uncertainty are named without drama.
3. **Atlas:** evidence, limits, and agency are made explicit.
4. **Private access:** the executive returns to an office, not an account portal.
5. **Today's brief:** the product begins with the decision in focus and the next move.
6. **Continuity:** evidence, decision, and follow-up remain connected on return.
