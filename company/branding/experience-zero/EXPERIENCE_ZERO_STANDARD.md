# Experience Zero Standard

## Selected identity

**The Quiet Observatory** expresses perspective before movement. The wordmark is Orendalis. The symbol is a horizon held within an open eye-like circle: orientation and attention without surveillance. The executive seal adds “Discretion · Perspective · Agency.”

Primary colors are obsidian `#080D12`, warm ivory `#F4F0E8`, mineral blue `#8EA7B8`, and restrained bronze `#C8A978`. Literary serif is reserved for consequential ideas; a humanist sans carries navigation, evidence, and action.

## Arrival

The arrival begins with one human truth: “Your career carries more than ambition.” It progresses from responsibility and uncertainty to the existence of a private place, then introduces Atlas only after the executive understands Orendalis. There is one primary action: Enter Orendalis.

## Private access

Authentication is an entrance, not a sales page. It says “Welcome back,” confirms that the private career office is ready, and avoids product or system language. Security errors remain direct and specific.

## First authenticated minute

The executive must see: where they are, the latest confirmed change, what needs attention, the opportunity in focus, Atlas's recommendation and confidence, and one next action. Empty state means “begin with confirmed context,” never an empty dashboard or fabricated content.

## Motion

Motion communicates arrival and state. Reveals are brief, cancel no action, and are fully removed under `prefers-reduced-motion`. There is no parallax, looping ambience, or decorative loading choreography. This follows the principle that motion should support the experience without overshadowing it ([Apple Human Interface Guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/motion)).

## Photography and illustration

Photography, when commissioned, should use architectural light, thresholds, considered distance, and real human presence without staged executive theatre. Illustration uses evidence lines, horizons, and constellations. Stock business imagery, handshakes, skylines, laptops, and artificial “success” poses are prohibited.

## Sonic identity — future design, not implemented

Sound is inappropriate until Orendalis provides explicit preference controls and can validate accessibility across devices. The candidate signature is a sub-one-second two-note cadence combining a warm wooden onset and clear glass resolution. It must be off by default, user-controlled, absent from routine navigation, and never used as the only success signal. No autoplay, ambient music, notification gimmicks, or productivity sounds.

## Language

Write with calm precision. Name stakes without dramatizing them. Prefer “evidence,” “perspective,” “question,” “decision,” and “next action.” Avoid “AI-powered,” “optimize,” “pipeline,” “command center,” “unlock,” “revolutionary,” and manufactured urgency.

## Governance

Any front-door change must preserve the ten-second distinction, thirty-second understanding, one-minute personal relevance, accessibility, and the authenticated/private boundary. Production publication remains a separate Founder Approval Gate.
