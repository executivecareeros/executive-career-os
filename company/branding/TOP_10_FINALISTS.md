# Top 10 Finalists

Scores are estimates out of 100. Trademark risk is preliminary only.

## 1. Orendalis — 88

- **Meaning:** Coined from the sound territory of *orenda* (inner force/agency) with a durable institutional ending.
- **Pronunciation:** oh-ren-DAH-liss.
- **Why it works:** Human, premium, distinctive, category-neutral, and fluent on stage.
- **Weakness:** Four syllables; counsel must examine the crowded Orenda family.
- **Brand / VC / memory:** 92 / 89 / 86.
- **Conversation:** “I work at Orendalis.” “The founders of Orendalis.” “Connect Orendalis.”
- **Trademark risk:** Low–medium.
- **Domain:** `orendalis.com` returned registry 404 on 12 July 2026; appeared hand-registerable, not reserved.
- **Logo:** An open circular path forming a subtle north-pointing aperture.
- **Personality:** Wise, enabling, composed, optimistic.
- **Colours:** Midnight navy, mineral white, warm amber.
- **Type:** Humanist grotesk with restrained serif editorial accents.
- **Tagline:** **Intelligence for what comes next.**
- **Mission fit:** Broad enough for career, education, founder, and business intelligence products.
