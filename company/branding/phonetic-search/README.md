# Phonetic-First Company Name Search

## Purpose

This folder records an independent, clean-slate search for a permanent company name that is easy to say, hear, spell, type, and remember. Earlier naming work remains preserved as historical research and did not influence this search.

## Board outcome

**No name meets the standard.** Five candidates reached the final review, but none combined natural founder use, low spelling friction, clean preliminary conflicts, and a convincingly durable identity. The founder should continue searching and should not proceed to checkout for any candidate in this report.

## Important disclaimer

Domain status can change at any moment and must be reconfirmed during checkout. Preliminary conflict screening is not trademark clearance. Before substantial brand investment, incorporation under a name, or trademark filing, obtain advice from a qualified trademark attorney in the intended markets.
