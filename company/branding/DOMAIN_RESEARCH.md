## Registration protocol

Before announcing any name:

1. Have trademark counsel clear the word mark in priority jurisdictions/classes.
2. Recheck registry status immediately before purchase.
3. Register the exact `.com`, common country domains, and defensive typo variants in one session.
4. Secure primary social handles and relevant app-store developer names.
5. Do not contact owners of registered domains until legal clearance and a confidential acquisition strategy exist.

> **Availability warning:** Domain availability changes continuously. No domain has been purchased or reserved by this research.

> **Legal disclaimer:** This research provides preliminary availability only. Final trademark clearance must be performed by a qualified trademark attorney before adoption. Final legal clearance is outside the scope of this research.
