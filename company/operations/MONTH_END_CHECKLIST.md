# Month-End Checklist

> Purpose: Close each operating month with complete records, reviewed controls, and explicit next actions.

## Finance and Assets

- [ ] Review expenses against receipts and approved commitments.
- [ ] Update the Founder Investment Ledger and Founder Time Ledger.
- [ ] Review subscriptions, renewals, usage, owners, and cancellation windows.
- [ ] Review recurring and one-time costs; identify unknown amounts.
- [ ] Preserve evidence in the approved secure financial location.

## Security and Infrastructure

- [ ] Review security alerts, access changes, MFA, recovery, and unresolved findings.
- [ ] Review GitHub ownership, permissions, branches, dependencies, and security alerts.
- [ ] Review Microsoft 365 licenses, administrators, aliases, health, and authentication controls.
- [ ] Review Supabase plan, usage, migrations, RLS, backups, and incidents.
- [ ] Review Vercel plan, deployments, domains, variables, logs, and incidents.
- [ ] Review domain, DNS, certificates, renewals, and recovery ownership.
- [ ] Review incidents, near misses, postmortems, and overdue corrective actions.

## Product and Company

- [ ] Review roadmap progress, assumptions, deferred work, and release readiness.
- [ ] Review customer and beta feedback, commitments, and support issues.
- [ ] Review company metrics and complete the monthly scorecard.
- [ ] Review technical debt and operational debt.
- [ ] Review company, product, engineering, legal, and operational documentation.
- [ ] Record material decisions and update owners and review dates.
