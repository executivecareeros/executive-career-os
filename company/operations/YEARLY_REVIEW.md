# Yearly Review

> Purpose: Create an annual record of company performance, stewardship, learning, and approved direction.

Year: `[YYYY]`  
Review owner: `[NAME/ROLE]`  
Approved: `[DATE/AUTHORITY]`

## Annual Assessment

### Vision and Mission

What remains enduring? What evidence requires refinement? Did company behavior match stated principles?

### Product

Record customer value delivered, capabilities retired or added, quality, adoption, trust, and unresolved product risks.

### Financials

Review revenue, costs, cash, runway, founder investment, obligations, assets, and professional accounting conclusions. Keep sensitive evidence outside Git.

### Founder Reflections

Record decisions, energy, strengths, mistakes, trade-offs, and responsibilities that should be delegated or protected.

### Customers

Summarize customer outcomes, feedback, retention, support, trust, and concentration without exposing personal data.

### Infrastructure and Security

Assess availability, incidents, recovery, access, vendors, data protection, continuity tests, and material improvements.

### Documentation

Review completeness, contradictions, stale records, decision history, ownership, and archival quality.

### Lessons Learned

State what changed the company's understanding and how future behavior will differ.

### Goals

Define a small number of evidence-based annual outcomes, accountable owners, constraints, and review dates.
