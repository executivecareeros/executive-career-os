# Company Principles

> Purpose: Guide choices when plans and incentives conflict.

1. Improve professional decision quality.
2. Explain recommendations and uncertainty.
3. Preserve history and provenance.
4. Earn trust before increasing automation.
5. Keep the executive in control.
6. Collect only what creates durable value.
7. Build for portability and long-term stewardship.
8. Prefer coherent systems to disconnected features.
9. Distinguish facts, observations, hypotheses, and opinions.
10. Optimize for lasting usefulness, not engagement for its own sake.
