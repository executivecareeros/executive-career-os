# Founders Agreement

> Purpose: Record the principles that govern founder collaboration before formal legal agreements are executed.

The founders commit to company-first decisions, candid communication, documented disagreement, responsible stewardship of executive data, and respect for the permanent nature of career history. Roles, ownership, vesting, intellectual property, confidentiality, departure, deadlock, and transfer provisions must be formalized with qualified counsel.

No founder may compromise user trust, silently rewrite history, or present uncertain intelligence as fact. Material decisions require explicit ownership, rationale, and entry in `DECISION_LOG.md`.

This document is an operating statement, not legal advice or a substitute for executed agreements.
