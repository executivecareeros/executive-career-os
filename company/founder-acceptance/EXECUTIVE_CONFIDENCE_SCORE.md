## Composite

**Overall confidence: 4.4 / 10**

The score is not an average of visual polish alone. Trust, truthful state, and completion of the core executive journey are release gates. Resolving the two Critical issues would materially raise every downstream dimension.

## Acceptance Threshold

Before inviting a first fictional executive:

- No Critical issues open.
- New authenticated workspaces contain no unrelated fictional personal records.
- The guided journey completes from Dashboard through Feedback without a direct URL.
- Logout and login restore the completed founder without reopening onboarding.
- Founder operational status is current and internally consistent.
- Desktop, tablet, 390-pixel mobile, and keyboard journeys pass.
