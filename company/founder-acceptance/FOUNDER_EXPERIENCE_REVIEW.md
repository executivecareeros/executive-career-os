## Founder Observations

- Inputs must survive validation mistakes and elapsed time.
- Completed stages should close and advance naturally.
- Examples should explain what belongs in every blank field.
- Professional history should accept reviewed CV/resume files.
- Atlas must present executive reasoning, not internal code.
- Stage-heading capitalization should be consistent.

## Executive Standard

The product should communicate uncertainty without anxiety, preserve evidence without overwhelming the executive, and make the next decision clearer without pretending authority or certainty.
