# Asset Review Checklist

> Purpose: Provide recurring controls for ownership, cost, security, renewal, and continuity.

## Monthly

- [ ] Critical provider status, billing failures, renewals within 90 days, and usage alerts.
- [ ] Domain, production, database, authentication, backup, support, and monitoring health.
- [ ] New subscriptions/accounts/assets added to registers; retired assets marked.
- [ ] Suspicious access, recovery changes, or unexpected administrators reviewed.

## Quarterly

- [ ] Full Company Asset Register reconciled with invoices and accounts.
- [ ] Account owners, MFA, recovery, backup admins, and least privilege verified.
- [ ] Subscription seats/cost/cancellation and vendor alternatives reviewed.
- [ ] Domain/DNS/email security and certificate status reviewed.
- [ ] Backup restore evidence and business-continuity contacts reviewed.
- [ ] IP contributor assignments, licenses, brand/trademark status reviewed.

## Yearly

- [ ] Physical/digital asset inventory signed by accountable owner.
- [ ] Business registration, accounting, insurance, IP, contracts, and certificates reviewed with professionals.
- [ ] Disaster-recovery and founder-unavailability tabletop exercise completed.
- [ ] Retention/retirement decisions and historical registers archived.
- [ ] Policy effectiveness and next-year budget approved.

Record reviewer, date, exceptions, owner, deadline, and evidence link for every cycle.
