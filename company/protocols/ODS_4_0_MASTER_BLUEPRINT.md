# ODS 4.0 – Orendalis Master Blueprint

> Status: Founder-approved permanent source of truth
> Effective date: 2026-07-18
> Authority: Founder
> Scope: All Orendalis product, engineering, intelligence, operations, and AI-agent decisions

## Mission
Build the world's leading Executive Intelligence Platform.

## Founder Principles
- Deterministic software first.
- AI only where it adds measurable value.
- Target AI Tokens Used: 0.
- Knowledge, evidence and trust should accumulate.

## Permanent Intelligence Layers
1. Executive Intelligence
2. Company Intelligence
3. Opportunity Intelligence
4. Industry Intelligence
5. Country Intelligence
6. Community Intelligence (Executive Rooms)
7. Knowledge Intelligence

## Program 1 – Global Employer Expansion
Employers first, opportunities second, countries third.
Expand canonical employers, preserve reusable identities, measure cost per employer.

## Program 2 – Atlas Executive Intelligence
Separate user preferences, verified facts, Atlas interpretation and unknown information.
Atlas recommends; executives decide.

## Program 3 – Executive Experience
Maintain premium UX with search, multi-select, fuzzy search, persistent preferences,
company pages, performance and accessibility.

## Program 4 – Company Intelligence
Treat every employer as a first-class entity.
Maintain canonical company profiles with identity, website, careers page, logo,
industry, headquarters, products, services, hiring footprint, confidence,
freshness and provenance.
Reuse everywhere.

## Program 5 – Launch & Operations
Monitoring, observability, rollback, security, audit logs and telemetry.

## Program 6 – Executive Rooms
Inspired by IRC/mIRC.
Not social media.
No followers.
No engagement algorithms.
No direct messaging.

Support:
- Permanent & temporary rooms
- Public/private/invite-only rooms
- Topics
- Owners & moderators
- Participant list
- Presence, Away, DND
- Join/Leave notifications
- Searchable history
- Archives
- Pinned messages
- Bookmarks
- Polls
- File sharing
- Calendar integration
- Moderation tools
- Audit logs

Modern services:
NickServ→Executive Identity
ChanServ→Room Management
Bots→Atlas Assistants

Executive Presence:
Optional role, industry, country, expertise, board interest, networking status.
No popularity metrics.

Room Intelligence:
Room Wiki, FAQs, best answers, Atlas summaries on demand, knowledge maps,
company/industry/country references. AI only on request or scheduled summaries.

## Program 7 – Opportunity Intelligence
Model hiring urgency, executive level, department, salary confidence,
remote flexibility, historical changes and opportunity relationships.

## Program 8 – Executive Reputation
Professional credibility only:
verification, board, enterprise/startup experience, industries, languages,
advisory, mentoring, fractional leadership.

## Program 9 – Executive Knowledge Graph
Connect Executives, Companies, Opportunities, Countries, Industries,
Skills, Technologies, Departments, Compensation, Hiring Trends,
Executive Rooms and Atlas.

## Program 10 – Trust Engine
Every fact includes source, confidence, freshness, evidence,
verification, history and conflict handling.

## Program 11 – Executive Workspace
Pipeline, interviews, negotiations, offers, compensation,
documents, recruiters, Atlas conversations, Executive Rooms,
calendar, tasks and goals.

## CTO Operating Model
Every sprint:
ROI review, architecture review, cost review, risk review,
performance review, founder priorities, recommendation, implementation.

## Token Economy
Check cache, fingerprints and deterministic methods before AI.
Cache accepted AI outputs.

## Observability
Measure everything:
scheduler, queues, workers, providers, latency, throughput,
database, errors and AI tokens.

## Founder Principle
If a feature promotes vanity or addiction, it does not belong.
If it improves executive decision making, trusted relationships or reusable
knowledge, it belongs.

## Long-Term Vision
Orendalis becomes the Operating System for Executive Careers and the world's
most trusted Executive Intelligence Platform.
