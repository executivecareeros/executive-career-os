# Product Roadmap

> Purpose: Describe capability horizons without sprint-level commitments.

## Foundation

Professional home, identity, Workspace, Career Ledger, Blueprint, opportunity intelligence, persistence, privacy, and trust standards.

## Intelligence

Atlas Memory, explainable reasoning, Career Capital, relationship and market intelligence, and verified professional evidence.

## Operating system

Executive rhythm, preparation, reviews, collaboration rooms, portable Career Passport, and governed integrations.

## Ecosystem

Professional editions, enterprise and partner offerings, verified trust graph, APIs, international products, and responsible autonomous capability.
