# Executive Journeys

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Executive Experience · Version: 1

| Journey | Inputs | Required outputs | Decision gates |
|---|---|---|---|
| Opportunity Review | Canonical opportunity; Opportunity Intelligence | Recommendation, explanation, evidence, confidence, next action | All five M6 gates |
| Employer Review | Canonical employer; Employer Intelligence | Recommendation, explanation, evidence, confidence, next action | All five M6 gates |
| Compensation Review | Compensation evidence; Compensation Intelligence | Recommendation, explanation, evidence, confidence, next action | All five M6 gates |
| Recommendation Withheld | Failed gate; available evidence | Explanation, evidence, confidence, Unknown, investigation request | All five gates disclosed |
| Evidence Insufficient | Available evidence; required gaps | Evidence, confidence, Unknown, investigation request | Evidence, confidence, trust |
| Conflict Detected | Conflicting evidence claims | Evidence, confidence, conflict, alternative, investigation request | Evidence, confidence, explainability, trust |
| Unknown Outcome | Decision summary; unobserved outcome | Decision summary, confidence, Unknown, next action | Evidence, explainability, trust |

Each journey declares mandatory disclosure of the evidence, confidence, Unknowns, conflicts, and alternatives relevant to that decision state. A journey is invalid when a required object, gate result, or disclosure is absent.
