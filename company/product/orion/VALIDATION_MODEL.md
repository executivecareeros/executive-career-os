# Validation Model

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Version: `orion-executive-validation-v1`

| Dimension | Measurement | Unknown when | Provisional success criterion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recommendation Accuracy | reviewed Supported ÷ Supported + Not Supported | no evidenced review | baseline ≥20; target set after production baseline |
| Recommendation Acceptance | Accepted ÷ Accepted + Rejected | no structured response | baseline ≥20; never interpreted as accuracy |
| Explanation Clarity | mean 1–5 rating | no rating | ≥4.0 across ≥20 |
| Evidence Helpfulness | useful selections ÷ all evaluated evidence selections | no evidence evaluated | ≥80% across ≥20 selections |
| Confidence Calibration | mean absolute error against reviewed validity | confidence or reviewed verdict absent | ≤0.15 across ≥50 |
| Decision Outcome | verified outcome distribution | outcome unknown/unverified | ≥80% follow-up coverage when observable; no inferred correctness |
| Research Time Reduction | baseline minutes − actual minutes | either value absent | positive median across ≥20 |
| Executive Trust | mean 1–5 rating | no rating | ≥4.0 across ≥20 |
| Unknown Handling | Clear ÷ Clear + Unclear | no applicable response | ≥90% across ≥20 |
| Alternative Explanation Quality | mean 1–5 rating | no alternative/rating | ≥4.0 across ≥20 |

Every measurement reports denominator and Unknown explicitly. Targets are validation rules, not live claims.
