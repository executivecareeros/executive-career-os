# Atlas Product Principles

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Atlas Product · Status: Binding

1. Improve executive decision quality, not information volume.
2. Reuse Orion; do not create parallel evidence or reasoning.
3. Separate observed fact from interpretation.
4. Cite evidence for every recommendation.
5. State confidence and its limiting basis.
6. Keep Unknowns, conflicts, alternatives, and failed gates visible.
7. Withhold recommendations when evidence or gates are insufficient.
8. Suggest only proportionate, justified next investigations.
9. Preserve the executive's decision authority.
10. Measure comprehension and trust through M7 before claiming product success.
