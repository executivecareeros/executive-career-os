# Atlas Reassessment Model

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Atlas Product / Decision Intelligence · Version: 1

## Entry conditions

Reassessment requires:

1. an explicit executive request and documented reason;
2. at least one new workspace evidence item;
3. completed review of every trigger evidence item;
4. citation of each trigger in the new M6 assessment;
5. the same canonical opportunity as the prior review.

## Preserved history

The reassessment record preserves previous and new review identifiers, recommendation state, confidence, Unknown count, trigger evidence, reason, request time, and completion time. It appends the new Atlas review and never modifies the old one.

## Prohibitions

Atlas cannot reassess automatically, reuse evidence already present in the prior review as a new trigger, change opportunity identity, or hide a recommendation/confidence change.
