# Atlas User Experience

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Atlas Product · Status: Canonical experience architecture

## Executive promise

Within approximately five minutes, an executive should understand whether an opportunity deserves attention, why Atlas reached that position, what evidence supports it, what remains Unknown, what conflicts exist, and what to investigate next.

## Reading order

1. Read the executive summary and gated recommendation state.
2. Review observed opportunity and employer facts.
3. Inspect evidence and confidence.
4. Challenge Unknowns, conflicts, and alternatives.
5. Compare reasons to pursue and reasons for caution.
6. Choose the next investigation or record a decision.

The experience is channel-independent. A channel may progressively disclose detail, but it cannot hide evidence, uncertainty, conflicts, failed gates, or recommendation limitations.
