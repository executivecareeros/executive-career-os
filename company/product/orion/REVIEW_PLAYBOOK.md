# Institutional Learning Review Playbook

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Governance Owner · Status: Active architecture

## Review procedure

1. Confirm the proposed observation describes Orion, not an employer or executive.
2. Resolve every evidence reference to its authoritative reviewed source.
3. Reject pending, anonymous, missing, or circular provenance.
4. Challenge the reasoning, alternative explanations, sample size, and causal language.
5. Narrow the scope until the evidence supports every claim.
6. Set confidence to the weakest supported level; preserve `Unknown` where necessary.
7. Select only permitted reuse targets.
8. Set review due or expiration time.
9. Record reviewer, review time, and reasoning.
10. Obtain explicit approval and record approver, time, and reasoning.

## Re-review

At review due or when contradictory evidence appears, stop reuse first. Then confirm, supersede, retire, or archive through an appended revision. Never modify historical recommendations or use a positive outcome alone as proof that a recommendation was correct.

## Validation checklist

- evidence and provenance are complete;
- review and approval are independently visible;
- scope and confidence are proportionate;
- history is immutable;
- expired or closed records are excluded from reuse;
- no automatic learning, tuning, or silent update occurred;
- ODS reserved approval gates remain intact.
