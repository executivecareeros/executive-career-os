# Atlas Intelligence Model

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Version: `orion-knowledge-graph-v1`

Atlas is a graph consumer, never a raw-connector consumer. Its bounded view contains canonical entities, evidenced relationships, assertions including conflicts and Unknown states, and evidence metadata. Raw jobs, raw metadata, source fact payloads, and reproduction references are excluded.

An Atlas Insight must cite existing graph evidence. Unsupported insights are rejected. Confidence is bounded by cited evidence and must state its basis. Atlas may compare evidence, expose conflicts, identify missing information, and explain opportunity/employer context; it may not invent facts, silently reconcile identities, suppress contradictory evidence, or mutate operational state.

This foundation enables evidence-backed opportunity explanations, employer intelligence, market comparison, and future career guidance without coupling reasoning to LinkedIn, ATS, Employer Portal, or any future provider format.
