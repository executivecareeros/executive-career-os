# Decision Timeline

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Atlas Product / Governance · Status: Binding architecture

## Events

The append-only timeline records workspace creation, stage changes, evidence addition and review, task changes, question changes, notes, and completed reassessments.

Every event has an immutable identifier, time, actor, description, evidence references, and stage transition where applicable. Chronology is a validation gate.

## Continuity

Timeline evidence does not replace source evidence. Notes remain executive context rather than evidence. Completed questions cite reviewed evidence. Atlas-originated tasks remain Suggested until the executive acts.

Historical events are never deleted, reordered, or rewritten when a recommendation changes or an opportunity reaches a terminal stage.
