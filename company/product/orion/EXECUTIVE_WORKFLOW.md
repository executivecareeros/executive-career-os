# Atlas Executive Workflow

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Sol · Status: Locally validated

1. A canonical opportunity opens with its current deterministic intelligence.
2. The product adapter creates an explainable Opportunity Review without adding reasoning.
3. The executive sees Recommendation Available or Recommendation Withheld, evidence, confidence, Unknowns, and conflicts.
4. The executive creates tasks, evidence, questions, or notes.
5. Evidence begins as Pending Review and cannot support a completed question or reassessment until reviewed.
6. The executive advances only through allowed decision stages.
7. Reassessment requires new reviewed evidence and appends a new review while preserving the previous recommendation, confidence, and Unknowns.
8. Pursue, Watch, or Skip records the review context visible at that decision.
9. The immutable timeline makes the latest state recoverable on a later session.

Atlas never changes a stage, accepts or completes a task, completes a question, or records an executive decision automatically.
