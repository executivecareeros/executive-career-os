# Executive Decision Journey

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Owner: Atlas Product · Version: 1

## Canonical stages

1. Opportunity Identified
2. Initial Review
3. Evidence Collection
4. Employer Research
5. Recruiter Contact
6. Interview Preparation
7. Interview Completed
8. Compensation Review
9. Negotiation
10. Offer Evaluation
11. Decision Pending
12. Accepted
13. Declined
14. Withdrawn
15. Archived

Stages move only through registered transitions. The executive supplies the reason and may cite reviewed workspace evidence. Accepted, Declined, and Withdrawn may move only to Archived. Archived cannot reopen silently.

The journey is not a mandatory funnel: evidence collection and employer research may iterate, and terminal decisions preserve the full preceding record.
