# Release Plan

> Purpose: Define how releases progress from internal confidence to customer availability.

1. Internal verification: integrity, security, usability, and documentation.
2. Private alpha: small founder-supported cohort and qualitative learning.
3. Pilot: defined use case, success measures, and support model.
4. Limited availability: repeatable onboarding, operations, and reliability.
5. General availability: proven value, privacy, support, and commercial readiness.

Release gates should be evidence-based. Dates do not override trust or quality criteria.
