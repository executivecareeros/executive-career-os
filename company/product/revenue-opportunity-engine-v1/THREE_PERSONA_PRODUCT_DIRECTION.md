# Three-Persona Product Direction

Status: Approved follow-up direction; not implemented in this mission.

Orendalis will evolve around three clearly separated personas:

1. Executive — discovers opportunities, evaluates evidence with Atlas, makes decisions, and preserves career outcomes.
2. Employer — presents verified company and opportunity context for appropriate executive audiences.
3. Executive Search — manages trusted executive-search mandates and candidate relationships. The product language is **Executive Search**, never “headhunter.”

## Implementation-ready boundary

The current Executive workspace remains the only implemented persona. A later product mission must define persona-specific authorization, information boundaries, consent, evidence ownership, cross-persona sharing, and commercial terms before Employer or Executive Search workspaces are built. No data is shared between personas by implication.

## Required next design outputs

- Persona journeys and success criteria.
- Workspace and permission boundary review.
- Consent and provenance rules for shared opportunity evidence.
- Employer and Executive Search value propositions.
- Phased delivery plan that preserves the Executive Opportunity Universe as the canonical opportunity layer.
