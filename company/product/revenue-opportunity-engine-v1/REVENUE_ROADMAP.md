# Revenue Roadmap

> Purpose: Sequence only the Opportunity Collection capabilities that materially increase the chance of acquiring the first paying executive.

- **Authority:** ODS 2.0 Product track and permanent Executive Opportunity Universe Directive
- **Status:** Active roadmap; original provider-specific boundaries are superseded
- **Date:** 14 July 2026
- **Scope:** First-paying-executive priorities; no estimates, commitments, or implementation authorization

## Revenue Hypothesis

A qualified executive will pay when Orendalis saves meaningful search time and reveals credible opportunities they would otherwise miss, while explaining why each opportunity matters and protecting confidential career context.

The engine must validate four things before expanding source count:

1. executives trust the provenance and coverage statement;
2. the universe contains enough relevant executive opportunities;
3. Discovery reduces manual searching;
4. Atlas explanations improve the decision to investigate or ignore.

## NOW — First-Revenue Collection Product

### 1. Trustworthy Intake Foundation

- source registry and eligibility gate;
- collection runs, health, freshness, and provenance;
- canonical Opportunity revisions and lifecycle;
- manual, URL, PDF, and recruiter-message intake;
- executive confirmation for imported artifacts.

**Revenue value:** an executive can place every opportunity in Orendalis immediately, including confidential ones.

### 2. Employer-Direct Universe

- Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Recruitee, Personio, and Workable collectors;
- bounded Company Career Site collection from employer-published `JobPosting` structured data;
- daily incremental refresh, bounded backoff, and source-local failure;
- no LinkedIn collection or arbitrary-site crawling.

**Revenue value:** credible automated breadth without waiting for restricted partnerships.

### 3. Quality Layer

- deterministic normalization;
- duplicate clusters with source variants preserved;
- Company identity resolution and bounded official context;
- stale/closed detection;
- source and field confidence.

**Revenue value:** one clean universe rather than another noisy job list.

### 4. Executive Relevance

- shared Discovery and Search index;
- faceted lexical search;
- Blueprint hard constraints and fit lenses;
- Discovery briefing with new, changed, conflicted, and unknown items;
- Atlas explanation, alternatives, and executive action.

**Revenue value:** converts aggregation into executive decision intelligence.

### 5. Revenue Evidence

- coverage and freshness dashboard;
- relevant-opportunity rate;
- time saved compared with the executive’s prior routine;
- opened, saved, dismissed, progressed, and decided outcomes;
- source correction and duplicate quality;
- Atlas usefulness and trust;
- cost and founder operating time per executive.

**Exit:** design partners confirm that the engine materially reduces search effort and surfaces decision-worthy opportunities with trusted explanations.

## NEXT — Expand Proven Value

- SmartRecruiters after its credential and provider-term gate is resolved;
- authorized Teamtailor sources;
- authorized Workday employer or partner access after its Founder gate;
- saved searches and company watchlists;
- company signal enrichment with approved evidence;
- outcome-based adjustment through transparent rules;
- source coverage selected from observed customer gaps, not logo count.

**Entry condition:** NOW metrics show relevance and paid interest, and the missing source is a documented reason for lost value.

## LATER — Partnership and Scale

- LinkedIn Talent partnership execution after contractual Founder approval;
- licensed Welcome to the Jungle or Wellfound access;
- independent treatment of Otta only if current source rights and identity justify it;
- additional ATS ecosystems;
- broad career-site extraction;
- multilingual unstructured extraction;
- predictive or generative matching;
- autonomous outreach or application.

**Entry condition:** repeated customer evidence, legal and provider approval, unit-economic justification, and maintained explainability.

## Build Order

1. Object, provenance, source eligibility, and manual imports.
2. Normalization and duplicate clustering.
3. Greenhouse collector and end-to-end run.
4. Discovery/Search index and freshness.
5. Blueprint lenses and Atlas explanation.
6. Lever, Ashby, Recruitee, Personio, and Workable collectors through the same contracts.
7. Company registry and bounded enrichment.
8. Revenue evidence with qualified design partners.
9. Add only the NEXT source that removes the largest observed coverage gap.

## Implementation Readiness Gates

Before code work begins:

- the source satisfies the standing delegation or receives approval at a reserved founder gate;
- legal/privacy review confirms collection and display boundaries;
- source terms are revalidated from official records;
- data retention, deletion, correction, and confidential import handling are approved;
- security review covers URLs, documents, email artifacts, hostile content, rate limits, and source failure;
- operational cost and monitoring limits are approved;
- acceptance criteria define relevance, freshness, deduplication, and Atlas explainability.

## Stop Conditions

Stop source implementation if access rights are unclear, the source requires an unapproved provider relationship, collection behavior changes, private or candidate data appears unexpectedly, costs exceed the approved limit, or relevance evidence does not justify expansion.

## Decision

The current revenue-ready foundation combines manual and confidential intake with seven employer-direct collection routes, including bounded structured Company Career Sites. LinkedIn and Workday remain mandatory Tier 1 priorities behind documented Founder Approval Gates. Expansion continues autonomously when it increases executive coverage and satisfies the standing delegation; provider count alone remains irrelevant.
