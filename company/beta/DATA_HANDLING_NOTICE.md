# Private Beta Data Handling Notice

> Purpose: Provide a plain-language founder-review draft describing private-beta data boundaries.

**Status: Draft — requires founder and qualified legal review before publication.**

Orendalis is evaluating an evidence-based opportunity decision workflow. The product asks for only the professional and opportunity information necessary for that workflow. Atlas uses confirmed information, identified claims, preferences, and recorded history to produce explainable decision support.

Atlas does not make decisions, predict hiring outcomes, apply for roles, or provide legal, financial, tax, or psychological advice. Missing information remains unknown.

Participants retain ownership of their information. Orendalis must provide approved access, correction, export, deletion, retention, consent-withdrawal, and account-closure procedures before real personal data is permitted.

Until those controls and legal materials are accepted, sessions use fictional or sanitized data. Do not send career files or sensitive information outside the approved product workflow.

Security or privacy concerns should be reported through the approved security or privacy contact listed in the final participant materials.

This draft does not establish legal readiness or contractual terms.
