# Private Beta Issue Severity

> Purpose: Apply consistent response expectations to beta defects and concerns.

| Severity | Definition | Examples | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| Critical | Active or credible harm to confidentiality, integrity, access, or irreversible history | Cross-workspace access, exposed secret, destructive overwrite, account takeover | Stop beta immediately; incident playbook; preserve evidence |
| High | Primary journey blocked or materially misleading without safe workaround | Cannot authenticate, assessment uses wrong user evidence, snapshot not preserved | Pause new invitations; owner assigned immediately |
| Medium | Workflow can continue with a documented workaround but learning or trust is impaired | Confusing unknown state, recoverable import failure | Triage before next cohort expansion |
| Low | Limited usability or presentation issue without decision or data impact | Minor copy or spacing problem | Record and review in normal cadence |

Privacy, security, and trust concerns may be elevated regardless of frequency. Severity is based on impact, not how many users reported it.
