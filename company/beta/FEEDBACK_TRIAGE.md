# Beta Feedback Triage

> Purpose: Convert structured participant feedback into safe, evidence-based founder decisions.

## Categories

Bug, Confusion, Friction, Missing Information, Trust Concern, Incorrect Assessment, Useful Insight, Feature Request, Performance, Accessibility, Privacy, and Security.

## Triage Flow

1. Confirm the record is isolated to its workspace and contains no unnecessary sensitive data.
2. Assign category and severity using `ISSUE_SEVERITY.md`.
3. Separate reproducible behavior from interpretation.
4. Link route, workflow step, product version, evidence, and expected behavior.
5. For privacy/security, stop normal triage and use the incident process.
6. Record owner, next action, status, and target review date.
7. Notify the participant only through the approved support process and only when follow-up consent exists.

## Prioritization

Fix safety, isolation, data integrity, recovery, and trust failures before usability improvements. Record feature requests without expanding Release 0.6 scope.

## Founder Review

Review after every session and before the next invitation. Company Control may show aggregate waiting counts only; it must not display private feedback text.
