# Design Partner Profile

> Purpose: Identify participants who can safely provide high-quality evidence during the narrow private beta.

## Required Characteristics

- Trusted, experienced professional or executive
- Comfortable testing an unfinished private product
- Willing to complete an observed founder-led session
- Able to explain confusion, trust concerns, and decision quality in detail
- Not dependent on Orendalis for an urgent career decision
- Prepared to use fictional or sanitized data until real-data readiness is approved
- Able to respect confidentiality expectations

## Exclusions

Do not recruit participants who require guaranteed outcomes, automated applications, legal or financial advice, immediate production reliability, accessibility support the product cannot yet provide, or real-data processing before lifecycle controls are accepted.

## Diversity of Learning

The initial 3–5 partners should expose different executive contexts without turning the beta into market research at scale. Seek variation in career stage, industry, geography, and opportunity type only where Orendalis can support the session safely.

Selection is based on learning suitability and safety, not prestige or marketing value.
