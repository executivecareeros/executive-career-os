# Private Beta Invitation Draft

> Purpose: Provide founder-review language for a future invitation after all go/no-go gates pass.

**Status: Draft — do not send. Legal and founder review required.**

## Subject

Invitation to a private Orendalis design-partner session

## Draft

Orendalis is conducting a small, founder-led private beta focused on one workflow: helping an executive examine a career opportunity through confirmed evidence, explicit uncertainty, trade-offs, and next actions.

The product is under evaluation. It does not make decisions, predict hiring outcomes, apply for roles, or replace professional judgment. Some capabilities are incomplete, and detailed feedback is expected.

The first session will be observed by the founder. Until Orendalis confirms its real-data controls, the session will use a fictional or sanitized profile. Participation is voluntary and may be stopped at any time.

Before participation, you will receive the approved beta agreement, privacy and data-handling notices, support route, and deletion/export procedure.

If you are interested, reply to the founder directly. Do not send career documents or sensitive information before receiving the approved secure instructions.

## Send Gate

The invitation may be sent only after the go/no-go checklist is approved and an accepted single-use invitation system exists.
