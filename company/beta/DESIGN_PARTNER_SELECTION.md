# Design Partner Selection

> Purpose: Provide a controlled, founder-approved process for selecting up to five private-beta participants.

## Process

1. Founder identifies a candidate matching the profile.
2. Record a minimal candidate code outside source control; do not place personal details in Git.
3. Confirm the candidate has no urgent dependency on the product.
4. Explain private-beta limitations before collecting data.
5. Confirm willingness to participate in an observed session and give detailed feedback.
6. Review data type: fictional/sanitized until real-data approval exists.
7. Founder records an explicit invite/no-invite decision.
8. Issue a single-use, time-limited invitation only through an accepted invitation system.

## Capacity

Invite sequentially, not all at once. Complete and review the first session before inviting the next participant. Maximum initial cohort: five.

## Stop Conditions

Pause selection for a critical security/privacy issue, failed isolation, failed restore, unavailable support, material trust concern, or mismatch between product claims and behavior.
