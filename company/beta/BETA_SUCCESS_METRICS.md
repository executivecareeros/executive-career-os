# Private Beta Success Metrics

> Purpose: Measure learning and decision clarity without vanity metrics or causal claims.

## Primary Outcome

**Decision Clarity Improvement:** participant-reported clarity immediately before and after reviewing the Atlas assessment on a simple 1–5 scale.

Report the individual change and cohort distribution. Do not claim causality or statistical significance from 3–5 participants.

## Journey Measures

- Onboarding completion
- Time to first opportunity assessment
- Blueprint sufficient for assessment
- Assessment completion
- Recommendation understood
- Evidence inspected
- Missing questions answered
- Next action selected
- Return within seven days
- Feedback submitted
- Trust concern raised
- User disagreement with Atlas
- Workflow abandonment point

## Definitions

Each measure requires a canonical definition, numerator/denominator where applicable, source, timestamp, data classification, and missing-data behavior before collection. A missing event is not automatically a negative outcome.

## Qualitative Evidence

Record the participant's explanation of what changed their understanding, where Atlas was wrong or incomplete, which evidence mattered, and whether the next action felt justified.

## Prohibited Interpretation

Do not equate completion, time spent, repository activity, recommendation acceptance, or return behavior with career success or product-market fit.
