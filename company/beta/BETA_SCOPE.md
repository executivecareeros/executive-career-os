# Private Beta Scope

> Purpose: Freeze the private beta around one evidence-backed Executive Opportunity Decision journey.

## In Scope

- Invitation-controlled authentication
- Narrow onboarding and Atlas Promise acceptance
- Reviewed professional-history import or manual entry
- Minimum viable Executive Blueprint
- One opportunity with company, role, location, work model, compensation, source, recruiter context, and constraints
- Deterministic Atlas assessment
- Evidence, conflicts, trade-offs, gaps, confidence, alternatives, and change conditions
- Apply, Wait, Reject, Monitor, Negotiate, or Network First next action
- Immutable decision snapshot and Career Ledger event
- Structured feedback and founder support

## Out of Scope

Automatic applications, live discovery, autonomous communication, calendar or WhatsApp automation, billing, public signup, enterprise/team workflows, hidden AI reasoning, public API, mobile apps, high-volume messaging, or broad provider integrations.

## Claim Boundary

Atlas supports executive judgment. Confidence reflects data completeness, not success probability. It does not predict hiring outcomes or provide legal, tax, compensation, psychological, or financial authority.

Any request outside this scope is recorded as feedback, not implemented during Release 0.6.
