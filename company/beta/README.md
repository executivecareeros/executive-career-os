# Orendalis Private Beta Operations

> Purpose: Govern founder-operated design-partner sessions for the Executive Opportunity Decision workflow.

This folder is the operational source of truth for Release 0.6 participant selection, scope, invitations, sessions, observations, feedback, support, data handling, success measures, go/no-go decisions, and exit criteria.

## Operating Rule

No design partner may be invited until staging acceptance, lifecycle controls, legal review, monitoring, restore proof, and founder acceptance are complete. Until then, all sessions use fictional or sanitized profiles.

## Documents

- `DESIGN_PARTNER_PROFILE.md`: suitable participant characteristics
- `DESIGN_PARTNER_SELECTION.md`: selection and approval procedure
- `BETA_SCOPE.md`: in-scope promise and exclusions
- `BETA_INVITATION.md`: founder-review invitation draft
- `FOUNDER_SESSION_GUIDE.md`: moderated-session procedure
- `OBSERVATION_TEMPLATE.md`: evidence capture without sensitive content
- `FEEDBACK_TRIAGE.md`: founder triage workflow
- `ISSUE_SEVERITY.md`: consistent impact definitions
- `SUPPORT_PROCESS.md`: support ownership and response flow
- `DATA_HANDLING_NOTICE.md`: plain-language draft requiring review
- `BETA_SUCCESS_METRICS.md`: learning measures
- `GO_NO_GO_CHECKLIST.md`: release evidence gate
- `BETA_EXIT_CRITERIA.md`: conditions for completing or pausing the beta

Review this folder before every invitation and after every session. Do not store passwords, tokens, raw personal documents, private support messages, or unnecessary participant information here.
