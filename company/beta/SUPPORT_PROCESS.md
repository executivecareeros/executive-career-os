# Private Beta Support Process

> Purpose: Define founder-owned support for a cohort of no more than five design partners.

## Channels

Use only the approved Orendalis support address after email infrastructure acceptance. Until then, founder-led fictional sessions use the pre-agreed session channel. Do not introduce WhatsApp automation.

## Intake

Capture participant code, route, workflow step, time, product version, severity, concise description, expected behavior, and follow-up consent. Do not request passwords, recovery codes, tokens, or full private documents.

## Response

- Critical: acknowledge immediately when observed; stop affected access and begin incident handling.
- High: same operating day during active sessions.
- Medium: next planned founder review.
- Low: acknowledge and backlog.

These are internal objectives, not contractual service levels.

## Escalation

Security, privacy, cross-workspace access, data loss, authentication compromise, or append-only failure uses the Incident Playbook. Provider incidents are linked to the vendor case without copying private message content.

## Closure

Confirm outcome, participant communication where consented, residual risk, and any documentation or test update. Never close solely because the error cannot be reproduced.
