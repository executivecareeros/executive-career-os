# Company Foundation

> Purpose: Provide the non-technical operating system for building Executive Career OS and Atlas.

- `founders/`: governance, origin, principles, decision memory, and long-term intent.
- `strategy/`: company, product, market, category, pricing, competition, and success strategy.
- `product/`: living backlogs, roadmap, release posture, and version history.
- `marketing/`: public narrative, website language, messages, taglines, and answers.
- `sales/`: private alpha, pilot, and demonstration practices.
- `research/`: executive interviews, feedback, and accumulated product learning.
- `investors/`: concise company and category materials for aligned capital partners.
- `operations/`: operating cadence, controls, risk, incidents, change management, readiness, and service operations.

These are living documents. Material changes should retain their reasoning and date in the founder decision log.
