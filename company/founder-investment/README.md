# Founder Investment Ledger

> Purpose: Preserve a permanent, factual record of the money, time, assets, and services founders contribute to Orendalis.

## Scope

This folder records founder investment for historical, governance, planning, and future reconciliation purposes. It is not accounting software, a tax ledger, an expense-approval system, or a substitute for invoices, bank records, payroll, legal advice, or professional accounting.

Accounting determines formal financial treatment under applicable law and accounting policy. This ledger records the historical fact and context of founder contributions, including items whose eventual accounting treatment is not yet known.

## Update Standard

Update the relevant ledger whenever a founder pays a company-related cost, commits to a recurring expense, contributes a material asset, or completes a significant work session. Record facts from receipts, invoices, contracts, provider dashboards, repository history, or contemporaneous founder notes. Never estimate an amount or date when evidence is unavailable; use `Founder to complete.`

Each update should preserve prior entries, cite a repository or secure external evidence reference where appropriate, and avoid credentials, payment details, invoice copies, or other sensitive data in Git.

## Ownership and Review

The founder or a formally designated finance/operations owner updates the ledgers. A second reviewer should verify material entries when the company has more than one authorized operator.

Review:

- Monthly for new expenses, subscriptions, time entries, and dashboard totals.
- Quarterly for recurring costs, assets, milestone costs, and missing evidence.
- Annually with the company accountant and legal adviser for formal classification, tax, capitalization, depreciation, and founder-equity treatment.
- Before financing, incorporation changes, founder transactions, or material financial disclosures.

## Documents

- `FOUNDER_INVESTMENT_LEDGER.md`: cash and non-cash founder investments.
- `FOUNDER_TIME_LEDGER.md`: founder work sessions and cumulative effort.
- `PROJECT_COST_DASHBOARD.md`: summary view derived only from completed ledger values.
- `RECURRING_EXPENSES.md`: subscription and renewal register.
- `ASSET_DEPRECIATION_NOTES.md`: future asset and depreciation policy notes.
- `MILESTONE_COSTS.md`: costs associated with major company and product milestones.
- `RETURN_ON_INVESTMENT.md`: future commercial return measures.
