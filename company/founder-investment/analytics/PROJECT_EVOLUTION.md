# Project Evolution

> Purpose: Trace Orendalis from product foundation through company and infrastructure formation using repository evidence.

## Evolution Stages

| Stage | Evidence | Measured outcome | Interpretation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Product inception | Initial commits and Sprints 1–4 | Next.js foundation, dashboard, design system, shared shell | The product moved from a homepage to a coherent application shell. |
| Core workspaces | Sprints 5–9.1 | Opportunities, companies, applications, agents, ledger, compensation | The system established its primary executive-career domains. |
| Intelligence architecture | Sprints 10–13 | Discovery, Blueprint, decision intelligence, knowledge | Source-agnostic discovery and deterministic intelligence boundaries emerged. |
| Durable platform foundation | Releases 0.1–0.2 | Repositories, identity, Supabase/RLS, auth, onboarding, import | The project added persistence, isolation, and first-experience foundations. |
| Atlas reasoning and productivity | Releases 0.3–0.4 | Memory, reasoning, signals, briefs, tasks, preparation | Atlas evolved from domain evaluation toward explainable decision support. |
| Experience unification | Release 0.5 | Design and interaction polish | Multiple modules were unified into one executive operating experience. |
| Company formation | Company, brand, history, and asset commits | Governance, strategy, brand decision, assets, launch planning | The repository became both a product record and a founder operating system. |
| Infrastructure preparation | Domain, GitHub, Microsoft 365, and DKIM records | Domain acquired; organization and email infrastructure established | Private-beta infrastructure is progressing under controlled change management. |
