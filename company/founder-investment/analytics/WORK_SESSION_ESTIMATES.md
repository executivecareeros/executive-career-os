- Work completed before files were committed.
- Research, reading, revisions, and discarded alternatives.
- Breaks and passive provider-provisioning time.
- Work performed outside the repository.
- Whether more than one person contributed effort.

Founder confirmation is required before any estimate becomes a historical ledger value.
