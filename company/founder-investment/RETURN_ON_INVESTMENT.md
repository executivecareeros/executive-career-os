# Return on Investment

> Purpose: Define the future structure for evaluating economic return without making premature forecasts or valuation claims.

## Future Measures

- Customer Acquisition Cost
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Payback Period
- Revenue
- Gross and operating profitability
- Cash contribution and burn
- Founder monetary investment
- Founder time investment

## Current Status

No return-on-investment calculation is available. Verified customer, revenue, cost-allocation, cohort, and founder-investment data do not yet exist in this ledger.

Future calculations must state their period, currency, source data, assumptions, treatment of founder time, and whether results are historical, forecast, or scenario-based. They must not imply an equity valuation or accounting conclusion without appropriate professional review.
