# Asset Depreciation Notes

> Purpose: Reserve a governed place for future asset-capitalization and depreciation decisions without making accounting claims.

## Asset Classes

- Computers
- Monitors
- Development and testing equipment
- Office furniture and equipment
- Other company-use hardware

## Future Policy Questions

- Legal owner and date placed in service.
- Business-use percentage and evidence.
- Original cost, VAT treatment, currency, and exchange rate.
- Capitalization threshold.
- Useful life, residual value, and depreciation method.
- Disposal, transfer, impairment, and founder-contribution treatment.

No depreciation assumptions or schedules are approved. A qualified accountant should establish the policy under the applicable jurisdiction before any value is recorded here.
