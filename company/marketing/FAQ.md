# Frequently Asked Questions

> Purpose: Answer common questions accurately and without exaggeration.

## Is this a job board or ATS?

No. It is an individual-centered Career Intelligence Platform.

## What is Atlas?

Atlas is the evidence, memory, reasoning, and preparation layer within Executive Career OS. The executive makes the final decision.

## Does Atlas invent career facts?

No. Confirmed facts, observations, hypotheses, and unknowns are kept distinct.

## Who owns the data?

The executive owns and controls their professional information, subject to clear workspace permissions and applicable law.

## Does the product automate applications or LinkedIn?

No. Direct application automation and LinkedIn scraping are not part of the foundation.

## Who is it for first?

Senior professionals managing complex transitions, opportunities, compensation, and long-term positioning.
