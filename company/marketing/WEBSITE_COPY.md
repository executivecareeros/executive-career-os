## Hero

**Your career deserves a memory—and an operating system.**

Executive Career OS helps you preserve professional history, prepare consequential decisions, and build long-term career intelligence with Atlas.

Bring opportunities, applications, compensation, decisions, relationships, and professional evidence into one private career workspace. Atlas shows its evidence, identifies what remains unknown, and leaves every decision with you.

You own your career data. History is not silently rewritten. Recommendations remain explainable. Portability and consent are product requirements, not policy footnotes.
