# Episode 1 V2 edit decision

## Decision

The original 84-second narration cut was rejected because it felt too slow and robotic.

V2 is the current approval cut and supersedes it.

## Corrections

- Runtime reduced from 84 seconds to 64 seconds.
- Narration changed from the slow Reed voice to the more conversational Eddy voice.
- Phrase pacing increased to 174–215 words per minute according to sentence function.
- Long dramatic pauses were removed.
- Every line now has a clean entry and exit; no dialogue overlaps remain.
- The picture and original score were tightened by the same editorial ratio.
- Score ducking reacts faster to the more energetic narration.
- Captions were retimed for the new master.
