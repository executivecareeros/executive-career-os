## Global 1000-per-country approach

The scalable approach is a country-weighted target registry, not a single global scrape. For each country, rank verified employers using lawful corporate registries, exchange listings, revenue/employment lists and official employer domains. Resolve each employer to a supported ATS or an approved first-party publication surface, then schedule only verified sources. Measure target employers, resolved career systems, active employers, canonical opportunities, freshness, executive-role density and unresolved source gaps independently.

This produces a theoretical target of roughly 200,000–250,000 employers once small jurisdictions and market size are handled honestly. It must be delivered in regional cohorts, starting with the G20, EU, UK, North America, Worldwide Remote and Türkiye, rather than claiming uniform coverage where a country does not contain 1,000 qualifying employers.
