# Quiet Ascent Brand Decision — 2026-07-18

## Decision

The Founder selected the original Option 5, **Quiet Ascent**, as the canonical ORENDALIS mark. Hidden-S variants and all other explored directions remain unselected design history.

## Meaning

- The open O represents ORENDALIS and a career that remains open to change.
- The restrained ascending path represents deliberate executive progress, not noisy or indiscriminate growth.
- The direction arrow represents clarity about the next consequential move.
- The mint origin point represents the executive as the source of truth and agency.

## Implementation

The shared application wordmark and public favicon use the same vector geometry. Existing Home navigation and accessible ORENDALIS naming are preserved.

## Acceptance

Founder design approval is recorded. Automated validation and live desktop/mobile deployment review complete the remaining brand gate.
