# Logo Options and Executive Identity Report — 2026-07-18

## Outcome

Three original ORENDALIS logo directions are ready for Founder selection. The signed-in product header now prefers the executive's confirmed full name over an email address on desktop and mobile.

## Logo decision

1. **Intelligence Orbit** — complete career context, evidence-led progress and the executive as the source of truth.
2. **North Star** — trusted orientation and clarity for consequential career decisions.
3. **Open Horizon** — a wider opportunity universe and a purposeful route to the next chapter.

The current production mark remains unchanged until Founder selection. All three candidates use the canonical wordmark and are intended to remain recognizable at application-icon size.

## Identity behavior

- Confirmed CV full name is promoted into the private executive identity on the next successful CV save without discarding existing profile fields.
- Existing profiles can resolve the confirmed name from their latest private CV context.
- Onboarding name remains a lower-priority confirmed fallback.
- Email is displayed only when no confirmed name is available.
- No name is inferred from the email address.
