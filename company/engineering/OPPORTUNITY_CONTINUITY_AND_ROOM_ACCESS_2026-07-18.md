# Opportunity Continuity and Room Access — 2026-07-18

## Outcome

- Atlas recommendations continue through the complete eligible inventory in descending confidence order, 12 per page.
- Executives can create Open or Invitation Only rooms in any declared language.
- Atlas-managed rooms remain Open.
- Invitation-only rooms use private, expiring, revocable, bounded-use links; secrets are never stored in plaintext.

## Validation contract

- No recommendation hard cap.
- Stable Previous/Next navigation and truthful result counts.
- Private rooms cannot be discovered or joined through the open-room path.
