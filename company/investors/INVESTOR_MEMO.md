# Investor Memo

> Purpose: Explain the company, category, strategy, and risks to aligned long-term investors.

## Thesis

Professional life lacks an individual-owned system of record and intelligence. Executive Career OS creates the Career Intelligence Platform category: longitudinal memory, explainable decisions, preparation, and portable verified history centered on the professional.

## Product wedge

Begin with executives facing high-value, complex career decisions. Build trust and persistent memory before expanding editions, integrations, and governed automation.

## Compounding advantages

Longitudinal career context, verified provenance, explainable reasoning, professional workflows, and a future trust ecosystem can deepen with responsible use.

## Business model

Individual subscriptions, premium professional tiers, governed organization workspaces, and future partner or platform capabilities—without selling personal career data.

## Principal risks

Trust, privacy, adoption frequency, category education, long sales cycles, premature breadth, and overreliance on model providers. The strategy addresses these through narrow initial use cases, provider independence, and founder-led learning.

## Current posture

The company is building and validating the foundation. Claims about traction, revenue, market size, or outcomes must be added only when supported by evidence.
