# User Interview Guide

> Purpose: Learn how executives make and remember career decisions.

## Opening

Explain that the conversation concerns their process, not a product pitch. Obtain consent for notes and clarify confidentiality.

## Questions

- Tell us about the last consequential career decision you made.
- Where was the relevant information stored?
- What did you wish you had remembered or compared?
- Who influenced the decision, and how?
- What career history is repeatedly lost or reconstructed?
- What would make a recommendation trustworthy?
- What information should a system never infer?
- What would make you stop using such a product?
- How do you prepare for interviews, offers, and reviews today?

Ask for examples. Avoid leading questions and feature validation disguised as research.
