# Commercial Readiness

> Purpose: Identify the evidence and operating capabilities Orendalis must establish before charging an executive customer.

- **Authority:** Commercial track under ODS 1.0
- **Accountable owner:** Founder
- **Status:** Assessment complete; revenue activation not authorized
- **Review trigger:** Completion of a Critical gate, material pricing decision, legal approval, or design-partner evidence

## Authoritative Records

- `REVENUE_READINESS_ASSESSMENT.md` — current commercial gate assessment and decision.
- `FIRST_PAYING_EXECUTIVE_ROADMAP.md` — ordered path whose sole outcome is a trustworthy first paid customer.

These records do not authorize pricing, customer solicitation, legal acceptance, payment-provider creation, invoicing, or sale. Each action remains subject to the ODS Founder Approval Protocol.
