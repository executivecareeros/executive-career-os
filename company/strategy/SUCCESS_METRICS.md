# Success Metrics

> Purpose: Measure whether the company improves careers responsibly.

Track decision usefulness, retained confirmed history, executive trust, recommendation explainability, preparation completion, repeat professional use, referral intent, and time saved on consequential preparation. Monitor data corrections, unresolved uncertainty, privacy incidents, and support burden as counter-metrics.

Revenue and growth matter, but must not substitute for evidence that executives make clearer, better-supported decisions.
