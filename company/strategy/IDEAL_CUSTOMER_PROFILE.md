# Ideal Customer Profile

> Purpose: Clarify who benefits most from the first product.

The initial customer is a senior executive or experienced functional leader managing consequential career decisions across opportunities, compensation, reputation, relationships, and long-term positioning. They value discretion, evidence, preparation, and control more than job-volume automation.

Strong early signals include multiple career options, international scope, board aspirations, complex compensation, or an active transition. The product is not initially optimized for high-volume entry-level job search.
