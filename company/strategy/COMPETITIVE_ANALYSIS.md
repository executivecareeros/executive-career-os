# Competitive Analysis

> Purpose: Frame alternatives customers use today.

| Alternative | Strength | Structural gap |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn and job boards | Discovery and reach | No private longitudinal decision system |
| ATS products | Hiring workflow | Employer-owned, not career-owned |
| Career coaches | Human context | Limited persistent shared infrastructure |
| Notes and spreadsheets | Flexible and controlled | Fragmented, weak provenance and reasoning |
| Generic AI assistants | Broad synthesis | Limited durable domain memory and governance |
| Personal CRMs | Relationship tracking | Not a complete career intelligence model |

The company should compete through coherence, trust, verified history, and explainable longitudinal intelligence.
