# Pricing Strategy

> Purpose: Establish principles for future pricing without fixing premature price points.

Price according to durable decision value, trust, privacy, and depth of professional memory—not message volume or addictive usage. Maintain a credible individual entry point, premium tiers for advanced intelligence and preparation, and distinct organization pricing for governed multi-party workspaces.

Never monetize personal career data through undisclosed advertising or sale. Validate willingness to pay through pilots before final packaging.
