# Definition of Done

> Purpose: Define completion for the bounded Operational Readiness reconciliation.

## Required Outcomes

- Company Control describes active staging and passed founder acceptance without claiming production or partner activation.
- Release 0.9 records distinguish operational-readiness completion from design-partner activation authority.
- New feedback records contain Release 0.9 plus the deployed revision when the platform provides it.
- Authoritative environment, provider, and integration registers agree on current staging state.
- Historical records remain preserved and are marked historical or superseded where necessary.
- Unknown costs, recovery state, monitoring, legal/privacy readiness, and delivery acceptance remain visibly unknown or blocked.
- Focused tests, lint, production build, documentation consistency, secret-pattern, and scope checks pass.
- The completion record identifies the evidence, limitations, and next approval gate.
- The working tree is clean after the completion commit.

## Completion Decision

Status: **PASS**, subject to the final validation evidence recorded in `OPERATIONAL_READINESS_REPORT.md`.

This decision does not authorize invitations, production deployment, public access, marketing, or new infrastructure.
