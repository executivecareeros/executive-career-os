# Founder Review Cadence

> Purpose: Keep the ten-partner program controlled, learnable, and supportable by a single founder.

## Before Every Invitation

- confirm candidate qualification and wave capacity;
- review unresolved incidents, Critical/High defects, support load, and recovery readiness;
- confirm current release revision and approved data mode;
- approve invitation expiry and delivery method;
- confirm time is reserved for onboarding and support.

## Before Every Session

- verify account and invitation state without viewing private content;
- confirm session purpose and learning questions;
- prepare fictional fallback data;
- confirm support and incident paths;
- avoid priming the participant toward desired answers.

## After Every Session — Within One Operating Day

- classify observations and feedback;
- identify trust, privacy, security, data, or journey blockers;
- record founder intervention and unresolved questions;
- decide whether the next scheduled invitation remains safe;
- communicate follow-up only with consent.

## Weekly Review

Review:

- active, invited, accepted, completed, returned, withdrawn, and closed partner counts;
- journey completion and abandonment points;
- decision clarity and Atlas comprehension;
- unresolved Critical, High, privacy, and security items;
- support load and founder capacity;
- product changes and validation state;
- lifecycle requests and response status;
- evidence quality and missing measurements.

Output: one page of decisions, owners, dates, and the next week’s maximum invitations.

## Wave Review

After partners 2, 5, and 10:

- review every success and stop criterion;
- compare learning questions and changes from the previous wave;
- confirm no participant harm or trust debt is being normalized;
- decide continue, pause, stop, or return to development;
- approve the next wave separately.

## Release Close

The founder reviews outcomes, evidence quality, operational load, recurring value, risks, legal/privacy readiness, and proposed Private Beta scope. The final decision is recorded even if the program stops early.
