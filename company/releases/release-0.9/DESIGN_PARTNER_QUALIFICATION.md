# Design Partner Qualification

> Purpose: Select executives who can safely provide relevant, high-quality product evidence.

## Mandatory Criteria

A candidate must:

- be a senior leader, executive, founder, or equivalent decision-maker with consequential career experience;
- have a non-urgent opportunity, career direction, or decision context suitable for reflection;
- understand that Orendalis is an unfinished, founder-supported product;
- be willing to complete the core journey and a return session;
- provide candid feedback about trust, clarity, usefulness, and disagreement;
- retain personal decision authority and not rely on Atlas as professional advice;
- accept the approved confidentiality, privacy, data-handling, and support boundaries;
- use only the data mode approved for the wave;
- be reachable through the approved support channel;
- have no conflict that compromises voluntary participation.

## Exclusions

Do not invite a candidate who:

- needs an immediate or guaranteed career outcome;
- expects automated applications, recruiter access, job sourcing, or hiring prediction;
- cannot safely use the supported accessibility experience;
- requires an unapproved jurisdiction, language, data type, integration, or legal commitment;
- wishes to upload confidential third-party information without authority;
- cannot distinguish product feedback from career advice;
- presents a material founder, security, privacy, or support-capacity conflict.

## Qualification Rubric

Score only after mandatory criteria pass. Use 0–2 for each dimension: 0 insufficient, 1 acceptable, 2 strong.

| Dimension | Evidence sought |
| --- | --- |
| Decision relevance | A real but non-urgent executive decision context |
| Feedback quality | Can explain why something is useful, unclear, or untrusted |
| Commitment | Can complete first session and return session |
| Trust fit | Values evidence, uncertainty, confidentiality, and agency |
| Learning contribution | Adds a context not already represented in the cohort |
| Operating safety | Fits supported language, jurisdiction, data, and accessibility boundaries |

A minimum of 8/12 is recommended, but the founder may decline any candidate regardless of score. A score never overrides a mandatory criterion or stop condition.

## Cohort Composition

Seek useful variation, not demographic theater:

- current role scope and career stage;
- industry and company context;
- geography within approved operating boundaries;
- opportunity type: external move, internal transition, portfolio/board, or deliberate wait;
- decision confidence and evidence completeness;
- prior use of coaches, recruiters, or career tools.

Do not collect protected or sensitive characteristics unless legally reviewed, necessary, consented, and secured.

## Candidate Record

Keep personal details in the approved secure operating system, not Git. Use a participant code in learning records. Record qualification outcome, founder decision, wave, data mode, consent state, invitation state, support owner, and withdrawal/closure state.
