# Design Partner Exit Criteria

> Purpose: Define when Release 0.9 may progress to Private Beta, extend for a specific question, pause, or stop.

## Ready to Consider Private Beta

All conditions must hold:

### Product and Trust

- Required Release 0.9 product corrections are accepted.
- Primary success thresholds are met or a documented exception explains a non-product denominator gap.
- Partners can explain Atlas’s recommendation, evidence, uncertainty, and their own authority.
- No repeated founder workaround is required for the core journey.
- Empty, loading, error, and return states remain truthful.

### Security and Privacy

- No unresolved Critical issue exists.
- No unresolved High issue affects isolation, authentication, invitation security, history, lifecycle rights, or misleading decision support.
- Data handling and legal materials match actual behavior and approved jurisdictions.
- Export, correction, deletion review, consent withdrawal, and closure paths are understood and sampled safely.
- Restore, incident, and account-recovery procedures remain accepted for the environment.

### Operations

- Invitations, support, feedback, triage, escalation, and closure work without direct database administration.
- Founder capacity is measured and sustainable for the proposed next cohort.
- Each wave has a recorded decision and unresolved risks have owners.
- Monitoring and provider dependencies are understood.

### Learning

- The founder can name the recurring value, trust boundary, main abandonment risk, and next product hypothesis.
- Negative and contradictory evidence is preserved.
- Private Beta scope is narrower than or equal to demonstrated capability.

## Immediate Pause

Pause invitations for cross-workspace access, secret exposure, history mutation, failed account recovery or lifecycle request, misleading Atlas certainty, material data/legal uncertainty, unavailable support, or a Critical issue.

## Extension

Extend only to answer a named unresolved question with a capped number of additional sessions. Extension does not authorize more than ten design partners under this release; exceeding ten requires a new release decision.

## Final Decision Options

- **Advance to Private Beta design:** criteria met; a separate package and founder approval are required.
- **Extend within Release 0.9:** a specific question remains and unused cohort capacity exists.
- **Return to development:** product or trust corrections are required.
- **Pause operations:** security, privacy, legal, recovery, or founder-capacity condition is unresolved.
- **Stop program:** expected value no longer justifies risk or effort.

Release 0.9 completion never authorizes public launch, billing, production expansion, automation, or marketing.
