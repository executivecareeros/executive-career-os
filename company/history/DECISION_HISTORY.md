# Decision History

> Purpose: Preserve important strategic decisions, alternatives, and expected impact.

## Atlas is evidence-first

- **Decision:** Atlas observations and recommendations require evidence and reason codes.
- **Reason:** Executive decisions should remain auditable and uncertainty visible.
- **Alternatives:** Generic generated advice; opaque scoring; model-first implementation.
- **Expected impact:** Higher trust, slower but safer capability growth.
- **Date:** 2026-07-11–12.
- **Status:** Active.

## Career Ledger is append-only

- **Decision:** Historical events cannot be overwritten or silently deleted.
- **Reason:** Corrections should preserve what was previously recorded and when.
- **Alternatives:** Mutable activity rows; application-level history only.
- **Expected impact:** Stronger auditability and Career Replay capability.
- **Date:** 2026-07-11.
- **Status:** Implemented and database-enforced.

## Career Passport belongs to users

- **Decision:** Professional portability is an executive right and product principle.
- **Reason:** Career history should not become provider lock-in.
- **Alternatives:** Closed export; platform-owned identity.
- **Expected impact:** Trust, portability, and ecosystem potential.
- **Date:** Foundational vision period; exact date [UNKNOWN].
- **Status:** Principle established; full capability future.

## Memory requires confirmation

- **Decision:** Atlas Memory derives only from confirmed records and distinguishes hypothesis from fact.
- **Reason:** Repetition is not proof of preference, and unconfirmed parser output is unsafe evidence.
- **Alternatives:** Automatic inferred profile; probability-only confidence.
- **Expected impact:** Explainable, correctable professional meaning.
- **Date:** 2026-07-12.
- **Status:** Implemented foundation, `dddf788`.

## Atlas explains recommendations

- **Decision:** Recommendations expose evidence, rules, gaps, alternatives, and what would change them.
- **Reason:** Consequential recommendations require transparent justification.
- **Alternatives:** Hidden ranking; unsupported prose.
- **Expected impact:** Better executive control and review.
- **Date:** 2026-07-11–12.
- **Status:** Implemented foundation, `c959fb3`, `412bc6c`.

## Career history belongs to executives

- **Decision:** Executive Identity, Workspace ownership, provenance, portability, and consent center the individual.
- **Reason:** Recruiting systems usually center employers and transactions.
- **Alternatives:** Employer-owned records; shared unscoped dataset.
- **Expected impact:** A trusted permanent professional home.
- **Date:** Foundational vision; identity implementation 2026-07-12.
- **Status:** Active.
