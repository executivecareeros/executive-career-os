# Photo and Media Archive

> Purpose: Define how visual and audiovisual company history should eventually be preserved.

## Future collection categories

- Early sketches and notebooks
- Whiteboards and architecture diagrams
- First user interface and product screenshots
- Early logos, typography, and identity explorations
- Launch and private-beta photographs
- Founder, team, office, and remote-work moments
- Conference and speaking photographs
- Press coverage and published profiles
- Product demonstrations, interviews, and event videos
