# Turning Points

> Purpose: Record decisions that materially changed the company’s direction.

## Atlas becomes the intelligence engine

- **Change:** From application tooling toward an evidence-led executive chief of staff.
- **Why:** Career decisions require context across an entire professional life.
- **Consequence:** Memory, reasoning, constitution, and trust boundaries became core.
- **Date/evidence:** Vision period; formalized in `30aec7f`, `5eef539`.

## Career Ledger becomes append-only

- **Change:** History is corrected through new records, not overwritten.
- **Why:** Career Replay and trust require historical integrity.
- **Consequence:** Append semantics, triggers, audits, and provenance shaped architecture.
- **Evidence:** `b9ffa10`, `73bba72`, `81f740a`.

## Product is separated from AI providers

- **Change:** Domain, memory, reasoning, and prompts are built without a live model dependency.
- **Why:** The product must remain coherent, testable, and explainable regardless of provider.
- **Consequence:** Deterministic engines and provider boundaries preceded AI integration.

## Career Passport is established as user-owned portability

- **Change:** Professional history is treated as portable user property.
- **Why:** A permanent professional home should not become a data prison.
- **Consequence:** Export, provenance, verification, and trust-graph ideas entered the long-term vision.
- **Status:** Principle established; full capability future.

## Opportunity discovery becomes source-agnostic

- **Change:** Atlas evaluates normalized opportunities rather than depending on LinkedIn.
- **Why:** Career intelligence should not inherit one platform’s limitations.
- **Consequence:** Connector boundaries, provenance, reliability, and Discovery Engine architecture.
- **Evidence:** `a32cb6c`.
