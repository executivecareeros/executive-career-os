# Lessons Learned

> Purpose: Convert company experience into durable operating knowledge.

## Technical

- Static validation cannot replace live database and authentication tests.
- RLS policies, PostgreSQL privileges, and workspace-integrity constraints must be tested separately.

## Product

- An executive operating system needs shared memory and language, not disconnected feature pages.
- Unknown information should remain visible rather than becoming convenient demo certainty.

## Business

- Product readiness, operational readiness, and legal readiness are different gates.
- A small trusted beta is more useful than premature volume.

## Founding

- Documentation preserves intent that commit messages cannot.
- A founder backlog can protect ideas without turning each idea into a commitment.

## Security

- Any suspected cross-user exposure is an incident, not a normal defect.
